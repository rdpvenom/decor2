About Velvet & Vine

We believe that stunning, soul-nourishing interiors should not require a design degree, an unlimited budget, or a full renovation. Velvet & Vine was created to distill the expertise of professional interior designers into simple, immediately actionable decor tips that anyone can use to transform their living space — one beautiful change at a time.

Our content focuses on practical, achievable improvements — the kind of changes you can implement in an afternoon with things you already own or can purchase for under fifty dollars. We cover every room, every style, and every budget, always prioritizing ideas with the biggest visual impact for the least effort and cost.

Every tip is tested in real homes, explained in meticulous detail, and written with zero assumptions about your prior design knowledge. We are not affiliated with any brands. Our recommendations are always independent and based solely on what works.
